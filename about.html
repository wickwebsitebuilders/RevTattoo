I'm a blackwork and fine-line artist working out of a private studio. I started drawing long before I started tattooing, and most of what I make still begins as a sketch at 2am — heavy black, thin lines, a little bit of rot around the edges.

My work sits where dark illustration meets clean technique. I'd rather do one deliberate piece than ten rushed ones, so I keep a single chair and a short book. If you bring me an idea, I'll draw it custom — I don't copy other artists' designs.

Comfort matters as much as the art. Sterile setup, honest pricing, and a flat answer if your idea won't age well on skin. What you wear, you wear forever — I treat it that way.
